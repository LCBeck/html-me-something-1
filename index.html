~ Providence Indeed! ~

Walking the streets of downtown Providence... my regular coder meetup.

The air is cool ... splendor in the fall!

Hungry, I turn to a young man: "Do you know any decent bagel joints 'round here?" ... he smiles.

"Do I???" he boisterously responds to my question... He directs me.

A tall & thin gentleman ... almost gaunt.

Blonde beard, thick rim glasses, black ear studs ... hip!

He's struggling to carry three large duffle bags ... odd!

"Oh hey man, do you like coffee?" ... ???

I reply in the affirmative ... he opens his luggage.

"Here take this!" and so I do ... he was in town for the coffee convention.

Providence indeed my Lord ... Providence indeed!!!
